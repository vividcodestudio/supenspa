Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

I'm new to massage — what should I expect?

No worries! Our therapists are trained to work with all experience levels. Just tell us during WhatsApp booking that you're new, and we'll provide gentle guidance and customize your session accordingly.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Absolutely. Send us a WhatsApp message with 24-hour notice and we'll accommodate you. We understand life happens and we're happy to work with you.

What should I wear or bring?

Come as you are in comfortable clothing. We provide all premium oils, hot stones, aromatherapy tools, and wellness equipment. Just bring yourself and your intention for healing.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash. Payment details are confirmed via WhatsApp before your appointment, before or after your session as per your preference.

Do you offer gift cards or gift packages?

Yes! Silver, Gold, and Royal packages make perfect gifts. WhatsApp us for custom gift packaging and a personalized message. Your recipient will receive a wellness experience they'll never forget.
