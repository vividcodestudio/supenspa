About This Ritual

Our Body Scrub & Renewal is a transformative skin treatment that combines deep exfoliation, detoxification, and intensive nourishment into a single luxurious 90-minute ritual. Using hand-blended scrubs crafted from premium natural ingredients, we slough away dead skin cells to reveal the luminous skin beneath.

The treatment begins with a full-body dry-brush exfoliation, followed by our signature botanical scrub application. After a warm shower (or steamed towel wrap), a deeply nourishing body oil is massaged into every inch of your skin, leaving you soft, glowing, and renewed from head to toe.

Ideal For

  • Those desiring softer, glowing skin
  • Pre-event or occasion preparation
  • Regular skin health maintenance
  • Individuals with dry or dull skin
  • Those seeking detoxification
  • Guests who love multi-step luxury rituals

Key Benefits

  • Removes dead skin cells for radiant glow
  • Stimulates circulation & lymphatic drainage

Your Experience Journey

Dry-Brush Exfoliation (15 min)

Begins with a full-body dry-brushing to stimulate circulation and prepare the skin for maximum absorption of the scrub ingredients.

Botanical Scrub Treatment (30 min)

Hand-blended premium scrub is massaged into every area of the body using circular movements, drawing out impurities and polishing the skin.

Rinse & Nourishment (30 min)

After rinsing, a luxurious body oil massage envelops you — sealing in moisture and leaving your skin luminously soft and hydrated.

Rest (15 min)

You rest as the final oils absorb, skin glowing and body deeply relaxed.
