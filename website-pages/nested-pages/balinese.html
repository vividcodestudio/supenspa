About This Ritual

The Balinese massage is a complete and holistic treatment rooted in the ancient Indonesian island of Bali. It combines deep and gentle strokes, skin rolling, kneading, and foot pressure with a carefully chosen aromatherapy blend to work on both the physical and energetic body.

Unlike single-technique massages, the Balinese approach seamlessly blends elements of reflexology, acupressure, and Swedish massage into one flowing experience. The result is deeply restorative physically, while also calming anxiety and lifting your emotional state.

  • Those desiring a middle-ground between light and deep massage
  • Guests wanting cultural authenticity
