About This Ritual

Our Couples Royal Retreat is designed for two people to experience the magic of premium massage therapy simultaneously, in the same private suite. Two expert therapists work in perfect synchrony, ensuring both guests receive identical, world-class treatment at the same time.

This 120-minute shared ritual is the ultimate gift — perfect for anniversaries, date nights, honeymoons, or simply a shared wellness retreat. The experience includes a personalised oil consultation, full-body massage, and a complimentary rose petal welcome and herbal tea ceremony.
