About This Ritual

The Swedish massage is the foundation of modern therapeutic massage — and at Supen Spa, we have elevated it to an art form. Using premium organic oils, our therapists employ five classic techniques: effleurage, petrissage, tapotement, friction, and vibration.

This versatile treatment improves circulation, reduces cortisol (the stress hormone), and creates a profound state of physical and mental relaxation. It is the perfect introduction to massage therapy and a wonderful regular maintenance ritual for sustained wellness.

Ideal For

  • First-time spa guests
  • Those seeking general relaxation
  • Anyone experiencing daily stress
  • Individuals with mild muscle tension
  • Guests wanting regular wellness maintenance
  • All ages and body types

Key Benefits

  • Reduces cortisol & stress hormones significantly
  • Full-body circulation improvement
  • Muscle tension relief & relaxation
  • Improved lymphatic drainage
  • Mental calm & better sleep quality
  • Ideal for regular wellness maintenance

Your Experience Journey
