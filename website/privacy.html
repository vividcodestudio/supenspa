Your Privacy is Our Highest Priority

1. Information Collection

At Supen Spa, we value the trust you place in us. We collect minimal personal information necessary to provide our premium services, including your name, contact details (phone/WhatsApp), and wellness preferences expressed during the booking process.

2. Absolute Confidentiality

As a sanctuary for elite clientele, we maintain strict non-disclosure of our guests' identities and session details. Your presence at our sanctuary is treated with the highest level of professional discretion.

3. Use of Personal Data

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Managing and confirming your private ritual appointments.
  • Personalizing your wellness experience according to your stated needs.
  • Communicating updates regarding your reservations via WhatsApp or phone.
  • Improving our internal service standards.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Supen Spa does **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share essential data with trusted software partners who facilitate our booking and communication systems, under strict confidentiality agreements.

We implement robust physical and digital security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. Our internal systems are audited regularly to ensure your personal information remains safe within our sanctuary's digital perimeter.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The latest version will always be available on our website.
