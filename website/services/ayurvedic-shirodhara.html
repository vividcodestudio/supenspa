About This Ritual

Shirodhara is a profound Ayurvedic treatment that involves the rhythmic pouring of warm herbal oil in a continuous stream onto the forehead, specifically targeting the 'third eye' area. It is famously effective for treating anxiety, insomnia, and mental fatigue. This ritual calms the central nervous system, promotes mental clarity, and offers a meditative experience of deep, silent stillness.
